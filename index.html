The Delightful Menu

Pasta Descriptions
Spaghetti Carbonara A classic Italian dish featuring spaghetti tossed in a rich, creamy egg-based sauce with crispy pancetta, garlic, and a generous sprinkle of parmesan.
Penne Arrabbiata Penne pasta cooked al dente and smothered in a bold and spicy tomato sauce, flavored with garlic, chili flakes, and a touch of olive oil.
Vegetable Primavera A delightful blend of seasonal vegetables, sautéed in olive oil and garlic, served over your choice of pasta for a light yet flavorful dish.
Lasagna Bolognese Layers of pasta, rich beef ragù, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella baked to perfection for a comforting, hearty meal.
Chicken Meats Descriptions
Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken Juicy chicken breast marinated in a zesty lemon and herb mixture, grilled to perfection and served alongside roasted potatoes and fresh vegetables.
Chicken Parmesan A golden, crispy breaded chicken cutlet topped with tangy marinara sauce and melted mozzarella, served with a side of pasta or garlic bread.
BBQ Chicken Wings Tender, crispy chicken wings tossed in a smoky, sweet barbecue sauce, served with a side of ranch dressing and coleslaw.
Beef Meats Descriptions
Classic Beef Burger A perfectly grilled, juicy beef patty served on a toasted bun with crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, melted cheese, and a side of golden fries.
Grilled Rib-Eye Steak A succulent 10 oz rib-eye steak, cooked to your preferred doneness, topped with garlic butter and served with seasonal vegetables and mashed potatoes.
Beef Tacos Soft corn tortillas filled with seasoned ground beef, lettuce, shredded cheese, and fresh salsa, served with a side of Mexican rice and beans.
Other Meats Descriptions
Grilled Lamb Chops Tender and juicy lamb chops, marinated in rosemary, garlic, and olive oil, grilled to perfection and served with a side of mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables.
Pork Schnitzel Crispy, golden-brown breaded pork cutlets, served with a tangy lemon wedge and a side of fresh greens and potato salad for the ultimate comfort meal.
Duck Breast with Orange Sauce Pan-seared duck breast, glazed with a sweet and savory orange sauce, served with a side of roasted root vegetables and wild rice.
Veggie Dishes Descriptions
Stuffed Bell Peppers Vibrant bell peppers stuffed with a flavorful mixture of quinoa, black beans, corn, and roasted vegetables, topped with melted cheese and baked to perfection.
Vegetable Stir-Fry A colorful assortment of fresh vegetables, stir-fried in a savory soy-based sauce with hints of ginger and garlic, served with jasmine rice or noodles.
Eggplant Parmesan Sliced eggplant breaded and baked until golden and crispy, layered with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese, served with a side of pasta.
